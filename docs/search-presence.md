## Research publication standard

Before publishing a research note, ensure it has:

- A descriptive title and permanent page URL
- Named author and publication date
- Referenced source material where factual claims are made
- Clear analytical scope and methodology
- The existing website disclaimer, with no investment advice or performance
  guarantee

After publication, link to the note from the Research page, share the exact URL
from official profiles, and request indexing in Google Search Console.
